Fashion Trend Forecast

A designer studies the upcoming fashion trends, patterns, colors, silhouettes and fabrics for the season. This is done through extensive research and analysis of trend forecast reports and fashion shows happening in the fashion capitals, where renowned designers showcase their collections. Trend Forecasting is a type of Fashion Research that analyzes market trends and buying habits to make educated decisions on how to move forward on designing a new collection. Trend forecasting focuses on telling a story through trends in silhouettes, design details, colors, fabrics, and trims.

HOW TO FORECAST TRENDS There are several methods that can be implemented when forecasting trends as a brand or fashion company owner.

1.Newspapers & Magazines - Off the shelf or by subscription only, fashion magazines and
newspapers are a great way to get a curated understanding of seasonal trends without having to break the bank.
Note that some of these may have an online option as well! Here are some that I recommend:

2.Fashion Forecasting Services - These companies create a curated collection of seasonal trends.
Some even provide a personalized trend forecast based on your specific brand needs.

3.Runway Shows - From NYFW to PFW runway shows across the world act as a catalyst for the upcoming
seasonal trends. While couture and avant—garde styles probably won’t be adopted into mainstream, the silhouettes, themes, and details might become a RTW trend.

4.Trade Shows - Attending trade shows allows you to see upcoming trends from suppliers and get access to curated presentations. I list some trade shows as a part of the Fashion Resource Directory, so you can check that out to get some examples.

Conceptulization

After an adequate amount of fashion trend research, a designer selects a theme or message that he wants to weave through his designs. These are translated to inspiration boards and mood boards from which the final theme for the collection is drawn.Fashion product conceptual design process is not only the process that designer give full play to the imagination via technology, but also a process which designers communicate with users to express users' fashion concept.

Fabric And Trim Selection
The next step to fashion clothes for the range is the selection of the right fabric to render the drape required for the silhouette. Trims and embellishments in the form of hand work or computerized embroidery are also selected to complement the body of the fabric.
The Color Story
Generating a color story is an important part of creating a fashion clothes range. Some designers select images or a series of images, to display their color story. Some use fashion trend forecasts which provide a variety of swatches, silhouettes and textile forecasts.
Range Planning
While the fabric and trim selection is being done, the designing of the trendy clothes starts. Sketches are drawn on paper or a computer and the garment imitation is constructed in muslin. This allows the designer to visualize the look of the garment and modify it till the desired output is achieved.
